Welcome to Delilah’s Dagger Tattoo and Piercing

Delilah's dagger has been through a lengthy refurbish and after 5 years we are proud of what we have achieved. Once known as Sydenham ink, we decided to branch away from the cliche title and go for something more universal. we wanted to build more than just a tattoo studio but a brand.

Our studio is all female owned and run. Housing a body piercer / hand poker and three tattoo artists.
